About Belfast

A former industrial powerhouse with a troubled past, Belfast has pulled off a remarkable transformation into a hip party town. The capital of Northern Ireland sits on the river Lagan and is the shopping, retail, educational, commercial, entertainment, and service centre for Northern Ireland and the seat of many of its largest businesses and hospitals.
